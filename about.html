Quality Policy

LManagement and staff of Blue Nagchampa. Mumbai are committed to serve with high quality agarbatti to the society. We being the pioneers of HAND ROLLED masala agarbatti, enjoy an edge over on other manufacturers. Our handmade agarbatti is being exported to USA, UK and many more countries in bulk quantities. We have our own handrolling units in Mumbai and Bangalore. Our third unit has been commissioned in Bangalore on 21st May, 2017. This new unit provides employment opportunities for more than three hundred employees at present. Being a women empowerment venture, all our handrolling units are managed by women employees. We have a full-fledged Quality Control Department and quality checks is being conducted in many stages of handrolling to assure that the good moved from our factory fulfil all international standards.
